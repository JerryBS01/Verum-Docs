Verum: Your Truth to Unbiased News

A Chrome extension designed to help you uncover bias news articles and blogs.

What is Verum?

Verum is a powerful Chrome extension that analyzes news articles and blogs for bias.

  • Bias detection
  • Easy-to-read insights
  • Source-based intepretation

github: https://github.com/JerryBS01/Verum-Docs

How Does Verum Work?

Verum uses advanced algorithms to scan articles and evaluate their credibility.

  1. Scans the article and understand the context ofthe article
  2. Checks the biasness of the content.
  3. Provides reasons on the biasness.
  4. Highlights where the biasness lies within the artical.

How to Install Verum

Installing Verum is quick and easy. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Go to the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Search for "Verum."
  3. Click "Add to Chrome."
  4. Pin the extension for easy access.

How to Use Verum

Using Verum is simple. Here's how you can get the most out of it:

  1. Open any news article or blog.
  2. Click the Verum icon in your browser toolbar.
  3. Click on the analyse button .
  4. Explore detailed insights and sources.
